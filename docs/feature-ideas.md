# JobLens — Feature Ideas

## High Impact (demo-worthy)

### 1. Resume Tailorer
Paste a job description + upload your resume PDF. AI rewrites your bullet points to match the JD's language and keywords. One of the most-requested job search tools.

### 2. Cold Outreach Generator
For any application in the tracker, AI drafts a personalized cold email or LinkedIn DM to the hiring manager — based on company, role, and your profile headline. Saves ~30 min per application.

### 3. Follow-up Reminder
If an application has been in the same column for X days, show a banner on the dashboard:
> "You applied to Stripe 14 days ago — time to follow up?"

One-click to generate a follow-up email draft.

### 4. Job Description Analyzer
Paste a JD, AI extracts:
- Required vs nice-to-have skills
- Seniority signals
- Red flags (equity cliff language, "wear many hats", etc.)
- Your fit % based on your profile's target role

---

## Medium Impact

### 5. Weekly Digest
A summary of the week's activity:
> "This week: 3 applications, 1 interview, 1 offer. Your pipeline is stalling at Phone Screen — 4 apps stuck there 10+ days."

### 6. Salary Benchmark
When adding an application, show a market range for that role + location (AI estimate or public dataset) so you know if an offer is below market before you even apply.

### 7. Interview Prep by Company
Before a round, AI generates likely questions for that specific company + role based on publicly known interview patterns.
Examples: Amazon = Leadership Principles, Meta = product sense, Google = system design.

---

## Nice to Have

### 8. Application Fit Score
When you add a card to the tracker and paste a job URL or description, AI instantly scores your fit 1–10 based on your profile. Shows a fit badge on each kanban card.

### 9. Rejection Pattern Analysis
Dashboard insight powered by your interview notes:
> "You're getting rejected most at Technical Round. Interviewers keep flagging system design in your notes. Suggested focus area: distributed systems."
